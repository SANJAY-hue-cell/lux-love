ABOUT US

About Us

Welcome to LUX LOVE, where we capture love, joy, and every magical moment in between. As passionate wedding photographers, we believe that every couple has a unique story to tell, and it is our honor to document your special day with creativity and care.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, LUX LOVE was born out of a love for storytelling and a deep appreciation for the art of photography. Over the years, we have had the privilege of working with countless couples, each with their own beautiful journey, and we strive to make every wedding we photograph a reflection of the couple's personality and love.

Our Approach

We take a candid and unobtrusive approach to wedding photography, ensuring that we capture genuine emotions and moments as they naturally unfold. Our goal is to make you feel comfortable and at ease, allowing you to fully enjoy your day while we work behind the scenes to create a timeless collection of images.

Meet The Team

Our team is composed of dedicated and skilled photographers who share a common passion for wedding photography. Led by Naruto , our lead photographer and founder, we bring a wealth of experience, technical expertise, and artistic vision to every event we cover. From the initial consultation to the final delivery of your photos, we are committed to providing exceptional service and creating memories that you will cherish for a lifetime.
